Nacho Cheese Sauce

This Cheese Sauce its easy and quick to do and it is delicious!

Ingredients

  • Shreded Cheese
  • Evaporated Milk
  • Cornstarch
  • (Optioal)Hot Sauce

Step By Step

  1. Combine all cheese and cornstarch in a saucepan over low heat.
  2. Then add in the evaporated milk and hot sauce, and whisk constantly until the cheese melts.
  3. Keep whisking for about 5 minutes, until the cheese sauce gets nice and t h i c c
  4. Add a little more evaporated milk if the sauce its TOO t h i c c.
  5. Serve it while its hot with some tortilla chips (honestly i just go with some Dorito's)
